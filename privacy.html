OffShift Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Privacy by design

OffShift does not upload, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data processing occurs locally on your device, and we never collect, sell, share, or transmit your data to any third parties.

Data we collect

Work shifts you enter manually in the app

Sleep sessions you log

App preferences and settings

All of the above are stored locally on your device only — never on a remote server

Apple Health integration

Sleep data is read only with your explicit permission

Used solely to display and analyze sleep info inside the app

Health data stays on-device and is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared

You can revoke access anytime in the Apple Health app

OffShift does not write any data back to Apple Health

Data storage & processing

All user data is processed locally using Apple system frameworks including SwiftData and UserDefaults

Widgets access limited summary data via Apple's App Group system for on-device display only

OffShift does not operate backend servers and never transmits data off your device

Third-party services

OffShift uses none of the following — and no third-party services ever receive your data:

Analytics services
Advertising networks
Tracking SDKs
External APIs
Subscriptions & payments

Payments are handled securely by Apple via the App Store

OffShift does not receive, process, or store any payment or billing information

Children's privacy

OffShift does not knowingly collect data from children

The app contains no content unsuitable for minors

Policy changes

If privacy practices change, this policy will be updated and the effective date revised. Check back here for the most current version.
